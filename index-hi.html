Kingsway College At A Glance

Kingsway College is a private Christian high school located in Oshawa, Ontario, Canada. Established in 1903, it offers both boarding and day programs for students in grades 9 through 12. Owned and operated by the Seventh-day Adventist Church, the college's mission is to reflect Jesus Christ and prepare students for His service. The campus spans 75 acres and includes facilities such as the A.E. King Fitness Centre, Buena Vista Hall, and the Holm Administration building. Kingsway College emphasizes a holistic education, focusing on academic excellence, spiritual growth, physical fitness, and social development. Notable alumni include singer Daniel Caesar and Member of Parliament Derek Sloan.
