Kingsway Extracurriculars

At A Glance Extracurriculars Mission Trips

Kingsway College in Oshawa, Ontario, offers a diverse range of extracurricular activities designed to enrich student life and foster personal growth. Key opportunities include:

  • Student Association:
    Organizes major school events such as the Mega Marathon, fall and Valentine's banquets, children's Christmas party, and Golden Gnome. Led by a Prime Minister, it comprises an executive body and a senate.

      Publications:

      • Cedar Trails:
        The annual yearbook, published since 1946, capturing memorable moments of the academic year.

      • Cedar Sentinel:
        A monthly student newspaper established in 1962, featuring contributions from students, faculty, and staff.

          Touring Groups:

          • Symphonic Choir:
            A longstanding choir performing at local churches and special events.

          • Concert Band:
            Established in the early 1960s, this band has a history of performances and accolades.

          • College Aerials:
            A gymnastics team promoting healthy, drug-free lifestyles among teens.

          • Drama Club:
            Founded in 1998, the club produces annual Christmas programs and spring plays, offering students a platform to explore theater arts.

          Athletics:

          • Varsity Basketball:
            Men's and women's teams, with the men's team actively participating in various tournaments.
          • Intramural Sports:
            A robust program featuring flag football, basketball, floor hockey, and soccer, encouraging faculty-student interaction.

          These extracurricular activities at Kingsway College provide students with opportunities to develop leadership skills, pursue personal interests, and engage with the community.
